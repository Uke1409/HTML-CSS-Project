There are many hidden messages and easter eggs in the show of "Naruto." Some made by the animators and some made by Kishimoto himself.

LOOK AT THAT! IT SAYS “NINJA” IN HIS HAIR??? WHY? Well... In the beginning of the show, Rock Lee is made fun of because he can only use taijutsu. He can't use any kind of ninjutsu. This made a toll on Rock Lee's dream to become a great shinobi. It also didn't help that his teammate Neji always tells him that he will never become a true shinobi. But right after Lee protects someone, this scene is being shown. Rock Lee showed us that what really makes a shinobi so great.
