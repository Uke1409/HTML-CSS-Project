Here’s a few quick recaps on some of the most interesting characters in Naruto. Itachi and Shisui Uchiha.

Think about eating your favorite food... The aroma... The texture of the food... The taste... SIKE. THIS IS ALL FAKE. You are in a genjutsu! It was Itachi, one of the strongest Uchiha. He is known for his insane genjutsu abilities. His most iconic justu include “Amaterasu” and “Tsukuyomi.” Amaterasu makes black flames that can’t go out in regular means. Tsukuyomi puts the other person in altered realities, where days of torture amounts to only a second.

Itachi was filled with talent as a kid. When he became 8 years old, he already had the mind of a hokage. He was a prodigy when it came to.... everything. He was wiser than almost anyone and had the skill of a legend. He hated the whole idea of war. There was no reason for it. That is why he killed everyone in his clan. They were about to start a Coup. People kept accusing the nine-tail attack on the Uchihas, and they had enough. Itachi didn’t want to see a civil war start in his own village. So, he had to kill all of his clan member... including his own parents. The only people he didn’t kill are Sasuke and his friend, Shisui... More info here.




BEHIND YOU! WAIT NO HE’S TO YOUR LEFT! WAIT NO HE’S ON YOUR RIGHT! Never mind, he’s long gone by now. Reminds me of this one character named Shisui. He was the Teleporter of the Leaf Village. He was known as “The Body Flicker.” His mangekyo sharingan allowed him to teleport to a destination, with almost no trace of him ever even being there.

His backstory is almost the same as Itachi. He hated war and death. He was loyal as can ever be to the village. He was a prodigy and taught Itachi some things too. But there are a few things that make them differ from each other. Shisui has a different ability with his mangekyo sharingan. His ability allows him to essentially teleport. And he died before the coup happened. Danzo (a high-ranking old guy) stole one of Shisui’s eyes. So Shisui escaped and gave Itachi his remaining eye and jumped off, so that Itachi would unlock HIS Mangekyo Sharingan. More info here.
